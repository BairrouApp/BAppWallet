# BairrouApp! MVP

## Overview

**BairrouApp!** is a territorial engagement platform designed to help local businesses build stronger relationships with their customers through digital incentives and programmable rewards.

This MVP focuses on validating a blockchain-powered coupon infrastructure, enabling businesses and service providers to distribute, manage, and redeem digital coupons through a simple QR Code experience.

Built on the **Stellar ecosystem**, the MVP leverages blockchain technology from day one while keeping the user experience simple and frictionless.

---

## MVP Scope

The first version focuses on a single core value proposition:

> Enable businesses to create, distribute, and redeem on-chain digital coupons through a secure and scalable blockchain infrastructure.

---

## Features

### 🎟️ On-Chain Coupon Wallet

Users can:

* View available coupons
* Save coupons to their wallet
* Track active and redeemed coupons
* Redeem coupons at participating businesses

Coupons are represented as digital assets powered by Stellar technology, enabling transparency, traceability, and future interoperability.

---

### 📱 QR Code Experience

Each business or professional receives a unique QR Code.

When scanned, users access a public profile page containing:

* Business information
* Products and services
* Contact details
* Available coupons
* Coupon redemption options

---

### 🏪 Business Profile Page

#### Business Information

* Contact information

#### Products & Services

A catalog of products and services offered by the business.

#### Available Coupons

Users can:

* View active coupons

## Stellar-Powered Coupon Infrastructure

The MVP introduces blockchain technology as a foundational layer from the beginning.

### Stellar Network

The Stellar blockchain provides:

* Fast settlement
* Low transaction costs
* Scalable asset issuance
* Transparent transaction history

### Soroban Smart Contracts

Soroban enables programmable coupon logic, including:

* Expiration dates
* Redemption limits
* Usage rules
* Campaign validation

### OpenZeppelin for Soroban

Security-first smart contract development using audited and reusable components.

### Stellar Wallet SDK

The wallet layer enables users to interact with digital coupons without requiring blockchain expertise.

The goal is to make Web3 infrastructure invisible while preserving its benefits.

## User Journey

### Customer

1. Scan a business QR Code
2. Open the business profile page
3. View products and services
4. Claim a coupon
5. Store the coupon in their wallet
6. Redeem the coupon at the business

### Business

1. Create a business profile
2. Register products and services
3. Create coupon campaigns
4. Generate a QR Code
5. Monitor coupon claims and redemptions
